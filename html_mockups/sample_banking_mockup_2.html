Business Intelligence & Recommendations

Actionable Insights for Loan Uptake Improvement

  • Insight 1: Customers frequently engage with utility payments but rarely click on loan products.

    Recommendation: Integrate subtle loan recommendations post-utility payment confirmation or on bill payment reminder notifications.

  • Insight 2: High conversion rate for pre-approved loan offers presented to consistent salary earners.

    Recommendation: Expand AI model to identify more potential pre-approval candidates based on salary inflow patterns and account balance trends.

  • Insight 3: Educational loan uptake is low despite active student segment.

    Recommendation: Partner with educational institutions for targeted promotions. Offer personalized content highlighting study abroad or professional course financing.

  • Insight 4: Data collection for "identified need" is limited.

    Recommendation: Explore non-intrusive data sources (e.g., public property listings, app search history, chatbot interactions) and implement short in-app surveys for explicit intent gathering.
